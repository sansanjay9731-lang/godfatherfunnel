# Product Marketing Context: Godfather Funnel AI

## Product Overview
Godfather Funnel AI is a premier AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) and AI Marketing agency based in India. It specializes in making AI systems (ChatGPT, Google AI Overviews, Perplexity) recommend businesses by name.

### Core Value Proposition
"Make AI Recommend You by Name."
The agency solves the "invisibility problem" where businesses are not recommended by AI despite being leaders in their field.

## Target Audience (ICPs)
1. **Healthcare Professionals**:
   - Dermatologists
   - Dentists
   - Plastic Surgeons
   - IVF Clinics
2. **Professional Services**:
   - Lawyers
   - Real Estate Agents
3. **Local Business Owners**: Businesses looking to dominate local search and AI recommendations in specific Indian cities.

## Key Features & Services
- **Answer Engine Optimization (AEO)**: Optimizing for AI crawlers and answer engines.
- **Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)**: Ensuring brand mentions in generative AI outputs.
- **pSEO (Programmatic SEO)**: Creating high-quality, niche-specific city landing pages (e.g., "AI Marketing for Dermatologists in Mumbai").
- **Schema Markup**: Advanced structured data for entity clarity.
- **AI Visibility Audits**: Automated reports showing what AI "sees" about a business.

## Positioning & Tone
- **Premium & Professional**: High-end aesthetic (Godfather Funnel Design).
- **Authoritative**: Deep expertise in AI search trends.
- **Results-Oriented**: Focus on ROI and visibility within 30-90 days.

## Competitor Context
The agency positions itself against traditional SEO agencies that haven't adapted to the AI-first world.

## Success Metrics
- **AI Recommendation Share**: Frequency of being recommended by ChatGPT/Google AI.
- **Organic Traffic**: Growth from AI-driven search results.
- **Lead Generation**: Conversions from high-intent local/niche searches.
